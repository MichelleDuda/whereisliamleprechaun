Where in Ireland is Liam the Leprechaun



Oh No! He's Got the Gold! Help Us!


In the heart of the Emerald Isle, the land where myth and magic come to life, a mischevious little leprechaun named Liam has stolen the legendary pot of gold from beneath the rainbow! Without this lucky pot o' gold all of Ireland's color and fortune will be gone forever. Can you help us find Liam before it's too late and Ireland's colorful landscape is gone forever?


How to Play


You will need to figure out where Liam is heading by listening to clues from witnesses.

In each county, three witnesses have seen (or know someone who has seen) Liam. Click on the Witness to hear and read the hint.

You will start your journey with 2 units of diesel.

If you select the correct county you will receive an additional unit of diesel.

If you answer the question incorrectly you will lose a unit of diesel!

If you run out of fuel before reaching Liam the game is over and Ireland is doomed!
