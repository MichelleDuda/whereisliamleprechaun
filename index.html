Where in Ireland is Liam the Leprechaun


Oh No! He's Got the Gold! Help Us!


In the heart of the Emerald Isle, the land where myth and magic come to life, a mischevious little leprechaun named Liam has stolen the legendary pot of gold from beneath the rainbow! Without this lucky pot o' gold all of Ireland's color and fortune will be gone forever. Can you help us find Liam before it's too late and Ireland's colorful landscape is gone forever?


Game Rules


You will need to answer a question to figure out which County Liam the Leprechaun is heading to!

Once we know where we're heading, a route will pop up showing us which counties we need to travel through to reach Liam.

You will start your journey with 3 lucky clovers.

In each county you will be asked a question about that county in order to move on to the next!

If you answer the question incorrectly you will lose a clover!

If you run out of clovers before reaching Liam the game is over and Ireland is doomed!
